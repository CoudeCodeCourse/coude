Contrary to the answers here, you DON'T need to worry about encoding if the bytes don't need to be interpreted! Like you mentioned, your goal is, simply, to 'get what bytes the string has been stored in'. (And, of course, to be able to re-construct the string from the bytes.) For those goals, I honestly do not understand why people keep telling you that you need the encodings. You certainly do NOT need to worry about encodings for this.
